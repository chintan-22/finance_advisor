## 📝 What You Can Ask

**For Recommendations:**
- "I have $10,000 to invest"
- "What stocks should I buy?"
- "I'm conservative, recommend something"
- "Best growth stocks?"

**Analyze Stocks:**
- "What about Apple?"
- "Tell me about Tesla"
- "Is Microsoft good?"
- "Compare Google and Meta"

**Learn:**
- "What is a P/E ratio?"
- "Explain dividends"
- "How does diversification work?"
- "What's market cap?"

---

## ⌨️ Commands

| Command | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `help` | Show help & examples |
| `profile` | View your profile |
| `history` | Show chat history |
| `clear` | Clear history |
| `exit` | End chat |

---

## 💡 Tips

1. **Be specific about budget**: "I have $5000"
2. **Mention risk tolerance**: "I'm conservative"
3. **Ask follow-ups**: "Tell me more about JPM"
4. **Clarify goals**: "I want dividend income"
